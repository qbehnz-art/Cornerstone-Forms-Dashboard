Local Security

This app is designed as a single-file local tool. It is not a cloud EHR and does not transmit PHI. Local lock helps prevent casual access; encrypted backup protects exported files when you choose that option.

Encrypted backup uses WebCrypto AES-GCM when supported by the browser. Keep exported files in approved storage only.

HIPAA Operations Checklist

  • Use managed iPads with screen lock and remote wipe.
  • Use unique staff accounts where the platform is hosted.
  • Do not store exported files in personal email or consumer drives.
  • Keep signed BAAs with any cloud vendor handling PHI.
  • Train staff on minimum necessary access and incident reporting.
